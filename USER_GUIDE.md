# Monvo — User Guide

> **Your money, finally making sense.**

---

## What is Monvo?

Monvo turns your bank statement PDFs into a clear financial picture — automatically. Upload a statement, and within seconds you'll have every transaction categorised, your spending broken down into charts, and AI-powered recommendations tailored to your habits.

No spreadsheets. No manual entry. Just insight.

---

## Getting Started

1. Go to the Monvo app and click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Check your inbox and confirm your email
4. Sign in — you'll land on the **Overview** page, ready to go

---

## 📄 Uploading a Statement

The first thing to do is upload a PDF bank statement. Monvo currently supports:

- **CIBC** — chequing accounts, savings accounts, and Visa cards
- **American Express** — Cobalt and other Amex cards

**How to upload:**

1. On the **Overview** page, choose your institution from the dropdown
2. Select the account type (Chequing, Savings, or Credit Card)
3. Drag your PDF onto the upload area, or click to browse for it
4. Hit **Upload** and wait a few seconds

Monvo reads the PDF, extracts every transaction, and saves them to your account automatically. You'll see a confirmation with the number of transactions found.

> **Pro tip:** Upload statements from multiple months for the best analytics and recommendations. The more history you provide, the smarter the insights.

## 💳 Transactions

The **Transactions** tab shows every transaction across all your uploaded statements.

**What you'll see:**

| Column | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| Date | When the transaction happened |
| Merchant | A clean, readable version of the merchant name |
| Category | Spending category (e.g. Restaurants, Groceries) — available for CIBC Visa statements |
| Amount | Green = money in · Red = money out |
| Account | Chequing, Savings, or Credit Card |
| Institution | Which bank the transaction came from |

**At the top**, three summary cards show you your total transaction count, total spent, and total received across all statements at a glance.

---

## 📁 Statements

The **Statements** tab lists every PDF you've uploaded.

**What you'll see:**

- **Statement Month** — the period the statement covers
- **Institution** — your bank
- **Account** — account type
- **Transactions** — how many transactions were extracted from this file
- **Status** — Complete, Processing, or Error
- **Uploaded** — how long ago you uploaded it

**To delete a statement**, click the trash icon on the right. This permanently removes the statement and all its transactions from your account.

---

## 📊 Analytics

The **Analytics** tab turns your transactions into three visual charts.

### Spending by Category
A horizontal bar chart showing your top spending categories, ranked from highest to lowest. The longer the bar, the more you spent there.

### Monthly Cash Flow
A line chart tracking two things over time:
- 🔴 **Spent** — total money going out each month
- 🟢 **Received** — total money coming in each month

Use this to spot months where you overspent, or see your income trends.

### Top Merchants
A horizontal bar chart of the merchants where you've spent the most — your top 10 by total spend. Great for identifying habits (and surprises).

> **Note:** Analytics charts populate as soon as you have at least one statement uploaded. Upload more months to see trends develop over time.

## 💡 Recommendations

The **Recommendations** tab is where Monvo's AI analyses your spending and surfaces personalised financial suggestions.

### How to generate recommendations

1. Go to the **Recommendations** tab
2. Click the **Generate Recommendations** button
3. Wait about 5–10 seconds while Monvo analyses your data
4. Your recommendations will appear as cards

> You need at least a few transactions uploaded for recommendations to work.

### What the recommendation badges mean

| Badge | What it means |
|-------|--------------|
| 🟢 **HISA** | You have a monthly surplus — consider a High-Interest Savings Account to put that idle money to work |
| 🟡 **Subscription** | A recurring charge looks like it might be a forgotten subscription worth reviewing |
| 🟣 **Credit Card** | Based on your spending categories, a specific rewards card could earn you cash back or travel points |
| 🔴 **Spending Alert** | A spending category has jumped significantly compared to your previous month |

Each card shows:
- A **title** summarising the insight
- A **description** with specific numbers from your own data
- An **estimated annual impact** (where applicable) — the real dollars at stake

### Acting on a recommendation

- Click **Done** when you've followed the advice (the card disappears)
- Click **Dismiss** to remove it without acting on it

You can generate fresh recommendations any time — Monvo will replace the old ones with a new analysis based on your latest data.

## A few things to know

- **Privacy first** — your statements are stored securely and are only ever visible to you
- **CIBC + Amex only for now** — TD, RBC, and other banks are not yet supported
- **More history = better insights** — one month of data gives you charts; three or more months unlock trend analysis and more accurate recommendations
- **Categories are richer on Visa** — CIBC Visa statements include Spend Categories; bank statements (chequing/savings) will show a dash in the Category column
